Chapter 2: A Law of Nature as a Shield

Instead of breakable math, QKD uses a fundamental law of the universe: the Observer Effect. You cannot measure a quantum particle without changing it. This isn't a technological challenge; it's the very nature of reality.

Security, Guaranteed by Physics

If an eavesdropper (Hacker) tries to intercept the quantum key, their act of measurement leaves behind undeniable evidence—like a burglar who must break a window to get in. The Doctor and Patient will see the disturbance and know the key is compromised.

1. The Quantum Channel

The Doctor sends photons with random quantum states (polarizations).

2. The Interception

A Hacker's measurement collapses the photon's state, irreversibly altering it.

3. The Public Channel

They compare settings. Mismatched or disturbed measurements are discarded.

4. The Secure Key

The remaining photons form a perfectly secret key, which is then used to secure data.
